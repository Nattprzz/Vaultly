# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Vaultly is used by people who track and review cultural media across games, movies, series, books, and concerts. They use it to inspect catalog pages, maintain a personal tracker, and browse context around creators, companies, and works.

## Product Purpose

Vaultly centralizes personal cultural tracking with a cached Supabase-backed catalog. Success means users can quickly understand an item or entity, decide whether it matters to their tracker, and move between related catalog surfaces without losing context.

## Brand Personality

Clear, focused, knowledgeable.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page spectacle, decorative card stacks, over-saturated gradients, and UI that feels like a generic media landing page. Catalog and entity pages should feel like trustworthy product surfaces, not promotional microsites.

## Design Principles

- Prioritize scannable information density over decorative presentation.
- Preserve category context while keeping the interface anchored in Vaultly's restrained blue system.
- Use familiar navigation and interaction patterns for catalog exploration.
- Treat empty and loading states as first-class product states.
- Keep user data surfaces separate from external catalog data.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, responsive layouts, and reduced-motion-safe transitions. Do not rely on color alone for status or meaning.
